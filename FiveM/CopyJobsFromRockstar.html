Extra things to do

⚠️Open Check Height

Avoid reaching checkpoints while on the ground

1.Select Checkpoint

2.Select Override

3.Check Height（Yes）

Race details

  • Title（Simplified Chinese or English）
  • Description（Simplified Chinese or English）
  • Length（The total distance between checkpoints must be greater than 1 km to save）
  • Route Type（Choose Point to Point. If there are no special requirements, do not choose Laps）
  • Map Type（Set to the author's name or abbreviation, such as ioio/RA/nape777/pino, etc.）
  • Image（The cover image can be left blank, or you can right-click and copy the image URL from the Rockstar job page and paste it.）
  • Force Vehicle（Set to BMX）
