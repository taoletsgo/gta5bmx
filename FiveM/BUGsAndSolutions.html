Some temporary bugs

⚠️Respawn location is on the ground（in race）

BUG description: When the checkpoint is on a red/green container, if the BMX position is higher than the checkpoint when passing the checkpoint, it will be respawn on the checkpoint, and if it is lower than the checkpoint, it will be respawn on the ground.

Solution: Use Menu (To be installed) to teleport and glide to the checkpoint.

⚠️BMX's Body Roll was loaded incorrectly when respawning（in race）

BUG Description: After falling during an errand/respawn after death, BMX's Body Roll was loaded incorrectly.

Solution: Please respawn again to get the correct Body Roll value.

⚠️Not easy to fall（in race）

BUG Description: When you load a race,BMX is in a semi-invincible state, but it is normal for the race not to be loaded.

⚠️Screen exposure in the creator

No solution. If the exposure is serious in the game, just re-enter the server.
